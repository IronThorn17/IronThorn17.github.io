About

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at St. Cloud State University, with an expected graduation in Spring 2026. My journey into software development began with curiosity. I started college without a specific goal or major, wanting to explore different paths. Eventually, I decided to dive into programming and game development, an old childhood desire.

Teaching myself the basics of Unity and game development was a turning point. I quickly realized that creating interactive experiences was not just fascinating, but something I could see myself doing long-term. This passion led me to choose Software Engineering as my major, focusing on building engaging and dynamic software solutions.

Outside of academics, I spend my free time developing video games in Unity, constantly exploring new ways to push the boundaries of what I can create. When I'm not coding, you'll find me playing guitar or spending quality time with friends and family.
